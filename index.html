About LaptopRepairLink

LaptopRepairLink was created to bridge the gap between students and laptop repair technicians at Daystar University. Inspired by the need for quick, reliable repairs, this app offers students a convenient platform to schedule services.

This project is part of the Holberton School Portfolio Project series. Learn more about the program here.
